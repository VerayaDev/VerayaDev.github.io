What is NEXUS?

NEXUS is a discord BOT made out of C# and was requested Augitesoul on the something rnadom discord server.

Click here to invite the bot, or would you rather make your own bot? click here!

Features as of version alpha 2.0.1:

>help - gives all commands and their info

>drama - Posts a random drama scenario from a PHP site.

>cuccd - Shares the image when a command is missing.

>Version Info - Gives info of the version you are running.

>Luck - A russian roulette type game, chooses a random scenario.

>Rate Faggotry - Rates the faggotry from 1 to 10.
